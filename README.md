## рџ§© What to put in your master resume

Your master resume should include:

- full name
- phone number
- email address
- city and state
- job history
- school history
- key skills
- tools and systems you know
- major results
- projects or portfolio links

Write each job entry with clear action words. Use short bullets that explain what you did and what changed because of your work.

Example:

- Supported 40+ users each week with account and software issues
- Cut response time by improving ticket triage steps
- Wrote help guides for common tasks

## рџҺҮ Best way to tailor a resume

When you tailor a resume, focus on the job post first.

Look for:

- job title
- required skills
- tools named in the post
- repeated duties
- words that describe the role

Then match your own experience to those points. Keep the most relevant items near the top.

Good matches look like this:

- job asks for customer support experience, so you show support work first
- job asks for Excel, so you place Excel work near the top
- job asks for project coordination, so you highlight planning and tracking work
